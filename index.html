Xueyang Wang

Ph.D. candidate, Tsinghua University.

I’m a fourth year Ph.D candidate at Institute for Network Sciences and Cyberspace, Tsinghua university supervised by Prof. Xin Yi. I obtained my M.S. degree on Data Science at Tsinghua university supervised by Prof. Lu Fang in 2022. Previously, I received my B.S. degree on Automation at the Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2019.

My research fields are Human-Computer Interaction, Adaptive Intelligence, and Usable Security in Extended Reality (XR), encompasses:

  • Egocentric Perception in XR Environments, including spatial understanding, hand/eye tracking, and emotion recognition in MR/VR interaction systems.
  • Multimodal XR Interface, emphasizing multimodal interaction and LLM-driven adaptive interfaces for immersive environments.
  • Security-Enhanced XR Systems, specializing in biometric authentication mechanisms and privacy-preserving sensing frameworks.
